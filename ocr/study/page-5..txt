Organization Visited
Name of the Organization: Sri Chirumurugan Autofinance
Purpose of the Visit
The purpose of visiting Sri Thirumurugan Autofinance was to understand the
existing process of managing employee data, loan applications, and repayment tracking. The
goal was to analyze the challenges in their manual system and explore how a dynamic. web-
based solution could streamline their HR and finance operations.
Discussion with the Organization
During the visit:
* Detailed discussions were held with the HR, loan processing, and administrative
departments.
~ The staff explained that most operations, such as maintaining employee records. loan
details. and payment schedules, are handled using registers or spreadsheets.
- They reported issues with delay in loan approval tracking, repayment management. and
lack of communication between employees and administrators.
~ Reports are prepared manually, which consumes a lot of time and often leads to errors.
