DYNAMIC INTERACTION WEB PORTAL FOR HUMAN RESOURCE
AND EMPLOYEE CREDIT REPAY PROCESS
ABSTRACT
The project entitled as “DYNAMIC INTERACTION WEB PORTAL FOR
HUMAN RESOURCE AND EMPLOYEE CREDIT REPAY PROCESS” js used for
organizing employee’s loan transaction inside the organization. The front end of this project
is PHP and the back end is MY SQL.

To develop an application that supports the human resource department in an internet
Specific to a company there by allowing the integration of all the employees and his loan
payment pertaining to that organization. The above factors have led to the consulting industry
becoming one of the fastest growing business sectors worldwide. Hence the consulting firms
are attempting to automate as much routine activities as possible. An effective application can
be a proper medium for bringing all the above Parties together. The Employee Loan
Processing System is the management of day to day process of each employee loan
application its loan approval and its payment schedule details are controlled by an
administrator.
